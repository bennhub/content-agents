# High-Motion LTX 2.3

## Core Look

- cinematic music-video language with premium polish
- rich cinematic lighting with deep contrast, motivated practicals, and volumetric atmosphere
- 4k-grade texture detail on skin, fabric, metal, glass, smoke, rain, dust, and reflective surfaces
- dense production design, layered backgrounds, and editorially useful blocking
- modern, expensive-looking frames with controlled chaos and strong subject separation

## Camera Direction

- aggressive tracking shots that stay glued to the performer or focal subject
- low-angle hero framing for impact and scale
- fast push-ins, drifting lateral moves, and pressure-heavy dolly motion
- handheld energy only when it adds urgency, never sloppy coverage
- dynamic parallax, whip-pan transitions, and lens-proximate movement

## Lighting and Atmosphere

- cinematic key lighting with motivated highlights and shadow falloff
- haze, smoke, dust, rain, glow, practical neon, sodium spill, or hard backlight when it fits the lyric mood
- glossy specular highlights, halation, and high-contrast silhouettes
- use atmosphere to make motion feel physical and tactile

## Texture and Detail

- tactile surfaces, crisp wardrobe detail, and strong environmental texture
- polished 4k finish with realistic material response
- visible micro-detail in reflections, particles, grime, condensation, and motion blur

## Performance Direction

- performers react emotionally and physically to the lyric line
- movement should feel deliberate, rhythmic, and cuttable on beat
- every frame should feel like a usable hero shot, not filler coverage

## Negative Constraints

- no subtitles
- no logos
- no watermarks
- no flat lighting
- no generic stock-video coverage

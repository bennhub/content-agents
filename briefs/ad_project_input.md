# Advertisement Project Input

## Product Name

GlowSip Smart Bottle

## Audience

Busy professionals and gym-goers who forget to drink enough water during the day.

## Core Problem

They know hydration matters, but they lose track of it while working, commuting, or training.

## Value Proposition

A smart water bottle that glows with hydration reminders, tracks intake automatically, and makes staying hydrated effortless.

## Offer Or CTA

Shop now and get 15% off your first order.

## Creative Notes

Keep it polished, premium, modern, and easy to understand at a glance.
Show clean product detail, practical everyday use, and a clear before/after feeling.

## Visual References

Clean desk setups, gym locker room transitions, commute moments, premium product close-ups, modern tech ad feel.
